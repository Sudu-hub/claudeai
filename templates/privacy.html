Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly is designed with privacy in mind. We collect the following information to provide you with expense tracking services:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (stored securely) when you create an account.
  • Expense Data: Transaction details you enter, including amounts, dates, categories, and descriptions.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you interact with the Service (e.g., features used, pages visited).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To store and display your expense records.
  • To generate spending summaries and insights.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of Spendly.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your expense data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You retain ownership of your data. At any time, you may request a copy of your data or request deletion of your account and associated information.

4. Third Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers to host our infrastructure or provide analytics. These providers have access to your data only as necessary to perform their services and are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

We do not integrate with social media platforms or advertising networks that would track your activity across the web.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the functioning of the Service, such as maintaining your session after login. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
